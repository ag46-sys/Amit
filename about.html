About Me

I am a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bhubaneswar. My research focuses on developing advanced non-thermal plasma systems for environmental applications, particularly for diesel engine exhaust treatment.

Over the past five years, I have worked extensively on the design and optimization of plasma reactors and high-voltage pulsed power supplies, aimed at achieving efficient NOx removal under realistic operating conditions. My work blends experimental development with a strong understanding of electrostatics and gas discharge behavior.

I am passionate about applying plasma science to real-world challenges such as clean fuel generation from plastic waste, syngas cleanup, and gas conversion.
